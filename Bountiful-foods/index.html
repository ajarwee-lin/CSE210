About Bountiful Foods
Thanks for coming to Bountiful Foods.
Bountiful Foods is an LA-based bakery serving up delicious sweet and savory treats with global perspective. Fan favorites include the fresh-made sandwitches, layer and cakes and pies. You can visit us at any time, our beach farmers' Market is open all days.
We're proud of what we do in our communities. We're here because you're there as our value customer. We give back to the communities that empower us.
At Bountiful, our bakers and chefs are people put their all in what they do because, they believed that, the best is here, and no where else. That is why We keep doing what we do for the last 57 years, around this great country. Thanks again for reading this, because of people like you, Bountiful still exist and we're here to always make you proud, We hope to see you again!
